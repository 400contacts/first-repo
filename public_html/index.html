400contacts

400contacts is a mentoring program for professionals. We match refugees and local professionals based on geographic location, professional interests and career plans.

When there is mentor and refugee match, we will send the refugee's profile to the mentor's email. The mentor can decide if they want to connect or not. The ultimate goal is to find a skilled job for the refugee in their new home country.

What is my role as a mentor?

Whenever there is a match, the mentor can help with a variety of things.

CV help

A great thing is to get a second opinion on CV, portfolio and LinkedIn profile. Is the skills and previous experience enough to land a job or is further education needed?

Industry expertise

Every industry is a little different. What companies should the refugee try to apply at given the background? What industry events are good for networking?

Fika

Perhaps the most important thing, grab a cup of coffee for a short while and talk about the situation. Topics of discussions so far have been anything from how to expand a company to the middle east or solve the problems facing African farmers.
